# Rule 10THA — Income Tax Rules, 1962

**Title:** Eligible assessee

---

**Eligible assessee.**

**10THA.** The "eligible assessee" means a person who has exercised a valid option for application of safe harbour rules in accordance with the provisions of rule 10THC, 45;)and—

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| (*i*) |  | is a Government company engaged in the business of generation, [46;)[supply,] transmission or distribution of electricity; or |
| (*ii*) |  | is a co-operative society engaged in the business of procuring and marketing milk and milk products.] |

---

45. Substituted for "and is a Government company engaged in the business of generation, transmission or distribution of electricity" by the IT (Nineteenth Amdt.) Rules, 2015, w.e.f. 8-12-2015.

46. Inserted by the IT (Second Amdt.) Rules, 2016, w.e.f. 17-2-2016.
